What is a UK export commodity code?

Commodity codes are numbers that classify the goods.

You must use the correct commodity code to:

  • complete export declarations
  • check licences
  • check if any restrictions apply

If you cannot find the right commodity code, you can ask HMRC for advice (opens in new tab). It can take several months for HMRC to classify goods.

! Warning It is your responsibility to ensure the commodity codes entered at the customs border are correct. Declaring an inaccurate commodity code at the customs border may result in penalties.
